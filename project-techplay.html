Learning Management System

TechPlay
Gamified LMS

TechPlay is a full-featured Learning Management System built to make education genuinely engaging. By integrating game mechanics — points, a virtual shop, and achievement badges — students stay motivated while teachers retain full control over content and progress.

The system supports separate Teacher and Student roles, each with tailored dashboards and capabilities. Teachers build modules and quizzes; students learn, earn points, and level up.

Features

What TechPlay includes

📚
Learning Modules

Structured course content organized into modules. Teachers create, edit, and publish; students access and track progress.

🧠
Quiz System

Multiple-choice quizzes tied to modules with scoring, feedback, and grade tracking per student.

🛒
Virtual Shop

Students earn points by completing lessons and quizzes, then spend them in the in-app shop for rewards and customizations.

🏅
Achievements & Badges

Milestone-based rewards to recognize student accomplishments and encourage continued engagement.

👩‍🏫
Teacher Dashboard

Full control panel for managing students, modules, quizzes, and monitoring class performance.

👨‍🎓
Student Dashboard

Personalized view showing enrolled modules, quiz scores, earned points, and available rewards.

🔐
Role-Based Access

Secure authentication with separate permissions for Teacher and Student roles, preventing unauthorized access.

📊
Progress Tracking

Visual progress indicators for modules and quizzes, helping students and teachers monitor learning outcomes.
